Upper Body

Sculpting a solid upper body is important for your overall health. To help you live a safe and balanced lifestyle, your workouts should include exercises devoted to exercising your arms, shoulders, chest and back.

    Benefits:

  • Improves posture and form
  • Stronger arms make it easier to perform everyday tasks
  • Increased arm strength will increase results in any workouts
  • Muscle burns more calories than fat
  • Keeping your arms pumped up will also give you a healthy heart
  • Arm exercises are one of the most flexible types of training
  • Speed + Stability - Solid shoulders and biceps will help you move at a faster pace and consequently burn calories at a higher rate
  • Reduces risks of injuries

There are four main muscles in your arms - biceps, triceps, deltoids and brachioradialis.
