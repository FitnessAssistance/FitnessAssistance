Image result for why do we need strong glutes Strong glutes are important for proper pelvic alignment, propulsion during running, and even single limb stance support. Strong glutes also help to support the lower back during lifting motions, and prevent knee injuries during lifting and running exercises.

    Benefits:

  • Improves posture
  • Counters the strain sitting has on our backs
  • Pain reduction and injury prevention
  • Improved endurance and agility
  • Increased bone density
  • Fat loss and fat maintenance
  • Stronger heart
  • Improved lower body coordination

There are five main muscles in your glutes - gluteus medius, gluteus maximus, tensor fasciae iatae, abductor magnus, and gracilis.
